Our Story

Built on fit, finished with custom tailoring.

Kingsmen began as a simple belief: menswear should be tailored to the man, not the other way around. Every suit, sherwani, and blazer that leaves our showroom is cut to measure, fitted by hand, and finished with the kind of detail that's increasingly hard to find off the rack.

As an official retailer for some of India's most trusted suiting and shirting brands, we pair premium fabric sourcing with in-house custom tailoring — so you get the label's quality and a fit that's entirely your own.

Carrying forward a legacy since the 1980s, Kingsmen has stood for timeless menswear, blending heritage fabrics with modern silhouettes to craft garments that last far beyond the occasion.
